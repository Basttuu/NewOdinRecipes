Smoky Sunrise Delight

A quirky twist on the classic breakfast cereal, replacing the crunch with a smoky touch. Imagine a bowl filled with the unexpectedâ€”cigarettes soaking in milk for a flavor that's entirely unconventional. This dish is an art piece rather than a meal, perfect for a surrealist breakfast table.

Ingredients:

  • A pack of cigarettes
  • 1 cup of cold milk
  • 1 bowl

Steps:

  1. Arrange Cigarettes: Place the cigarettes vertically in a bowl, standing up like cereal sticks.
  2. Add Milk: Pour the cold milk over the cigarettes, allowing it to soak slightly.
  3. Serve Immediately: Present as a whimsical art piece rather than a consumable dish.
